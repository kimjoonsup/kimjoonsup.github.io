## Research Interests

## Current Work

### Two-Sided Contextual Bandits with UCB

M.S. thesis / manuscript in preparation with [Prof. Min-hwan Oh](https://minoh.io). This project focuses on UCB-type methods for two-sided contextual bandit settings, with motivation from preference learning problems in online matching platforms.

## Previous Research Experience

As an undergraduate research assistant at Yonsei University's Computational Science and Engineering Lab, I studied theoretical models of epidemic spread and intervention strategies, including how policy design and vaccination strategies affect infection outcomes and economic costs.
